CATS

Siamese Cat

Lifespan: 15-20 years

Characteristics:

  • Distinctive color points on the ears, face, paws, and tail
  • Blue almond-shaped eyes
  • Short coat with a sleek appearance

The Siamese cat is known for its vocal nature and social personality.

American Curl

American Curl cats are wonderful

Characteristics:

  • Ears curl towards the back of its head
  • Intelligent, playful, and curious
  • Form strong attachments to their owners.

Calico

Calico cats are wonderful

Characteristics:

  • World's most colorful cats.
  • Sweet, loving, and loyal
